#  Students Hub

**Students Hub** is a web-based platform designed to connect students with **job opportunities, internships, and industrial attachments** across the country.  
It provides a simple, transparent, and user-friendly system where students can find work, showcase their skills, and build real professional experience ‚Äî all in one place.

Unlike complicated job portals, Students Hub focuses on **clarity, trust, and functionality**, enabling seamless interaction between students, companies, government institutions, and individual employers.

---

##  Concept Overview

**Concept by:** Ben Muchilwa  

Students Hub was created to solve a common problem:  
> Students struggle to access verified job opportunities, while employers struggle to reach skilled, motivated students.

This platform bridges that gap by offering a centralized digital space built specifically for students and institutions that work with them.

---

##  Purpose & Goals

- Bridge the gap between students and employers  
- Provide a central platform for:
  - Part-time jobs
  - Internships
  - Attachments
  - Full-time opportunities
- Empower students to:
  - Showcase skills
  - Upload professional documents
  - Build a verified work record
- Give companies and government institutions a trusted space to post **verified opportunities**
- Make the job search process **simpler, faster, and more transparent**

##  Core Features

###  Student Features

- **Dashboard**
  - View available jobs
  - Track applications
  - Receive notifications and updates

- **Skills Section**
  - List skills such as:
    - Writing
    - Coding
    - Catering
    - Graphic design
    - Technical work
  - Skills visible to employers

- **Document Upload**
  - Upload CVs
  - Certificates
  - Recommendation letters
  - Documents accessible to employers

- **Profile Page**
  - Profile photo
  - Contact information
  - Personal and academic details

- **Earnings Section**
  - Track income earned through the platform

- **Credits Section**
  - Verified record of completed jobs
  - Builds trust and professional history

- **Company Interaction**
  - Visit company pages
  - View job posts, updates, and recruitment notices

---

###  Company Features

- **Company Pages**
  - Dedicated profile pages
  - Post jobs and updates

- **Verification Badges**
  - Verified companies gain trust from students

- **Job Posting & Updates**
  - Easy job posting
  - Appear directly on student dashboards

- **Search & Filter**
  - Filter students by:
    - University
    - Skill
    - Location

- **Online Status**
  - See which students are active

---

###  Government Features

- **Verified Government Pages**
  - Official attachment, internship, and job postings

- **Public Updates**
  - Real-time notices visible to all students

---

###  Individual Employers

- **Single-Person Job Posting**
  - Post short-term or one-time jobs

- **Filtered Listings**
  - Separate views for:
    - Companies
